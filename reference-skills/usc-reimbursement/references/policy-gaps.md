# Policy Gaps

Use this file to capture USC-specific rules that still need confirmation. The point is to avoid rediscovering the same uncertainty in every case.

## Open questions

- Which reimbursement systems or forms are used by each USC school or department involved in this repo?
- What are the exact timing deadlines for reimbursement submission after purchase or travel end date?
- Which expense categories require prior approval, and what evidence counts as sufficient approval?
- What extra documents are required for meals, guest travel, or student reimbursements?
- Which funding sources impose stricter rules than the default departmental process?
- What are the allowed substitutes when an itemized receipt is missing?

## How to update

When a rule is confirmed, replace the question with:

- the rule in one sentence
- the date it was confirmed
- the source of truth, such as department guidance or policy URL
- any exceptions or department-specific variation

## Example entry format

### Meal attendee documentation

- Rule: [fill in]
- Confirmed on: [fill in]
- Source: [fill in]
- Applies to: [fill in]
- Notes: [fill in]
